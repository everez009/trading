Key Adjustments for Mag 7 Stocks

1. Higher Volatility Stocks (NVDA, TSLA, AMZN)

  • Use shorter lengths & higher multipliers (TRF Fast Mult = 2.0+)
  • Wider HAT squeeze % (0.3-0.35%) to avoid false signals

2. Stable Giants (AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL)

  • Smoother settings (longer HAT/TRF periods)
  • Tighter VIDYA bands (1.4-1.6x ATR)

3. Momentum-Driven (META, NVDA, TSLA)

  • VIDYA momentum = 20+ to catch sharp moves
  • Allow more pivots (2-3 bars) for swing entries

Best Timeframes for Mag 7 Stocks
